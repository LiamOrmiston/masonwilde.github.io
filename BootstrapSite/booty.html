FAQ

Who is eligible to apply?

All students currently enrolled at a university are encouraged to apply! You must be 18 or older by February 17, 2017.

How much does it cost?

$0.00. JayHacks is free to attend!

How much experience is required?

No experience is required! We are expecting a wide range of experience levels and interests.

What should I build?

We will help you generate project ideas at the beginning of JayHacks! Throughout the weekend, we will offer workshops and talks to help you hone in your skills. Sponsors will also be sending engineers to JayHacks to offer any advice or help that you may need.

Will there be hardware?

Yes! We will provide a variety of hardware for those interested in hardware hacking. Please let us know if there is something specific you would like for your project. A list of available hardware will be sent out closer to the event.

Do I need to have a team to attend?

We welcome both teams and individuals to our event! If you wish to join a team, but don't have one prior to the event, we will have a special time for fellow hackers to meet you and potentially form a team with you!

Do I have to submit a project?

We encourage you to submit your work, but we do not require submissions. Each team is permitted one submission that must be received by 10:00 AM on Sunday, February 19, 2017. We will have a project fair for you to show off your submissions to judges and fellow hackers. We will then have an awards ceremony to announce and hand out prizes to the winning teams.

Can I use an existing project?

While we can’t prevent you from submitting a previously made project, the hackathon spirit is to present only what is made during the event.

Will there be free meals, snacks, and drinks?

Yes! We will provide all meals, snacks, and drinks. We will have a variety of options. Please mention on the application if you have any dietary restrictions.

What should I bring?

A government and school ID will be necessary for check in. You should bring a change of clothing (or two), personal items, a laptop and charger, and a sleeping bag and pillow.

What if my question isn't listed here?

Just email your question to contact@jayhacks.io and we will prepare a custom-made answer for you!
